ABOUT ME

Hi! I am a master student at School of Physics, Zhejiang University and an incoming PhD student at School of Science and Engineering. I feel very fortunate to have had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Professor Lih-King Lim since 2020. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Department of Energy and Power at Guangdong University of Technology in 2020. During my undergraduate period, I work with Professor Kaige Hu in the condensed matter theory group from 2018 to 2020 and Professor Weitong Cai in the catalytic chemistry group from 2017 to 2019.

In recent years, I focused on exploring the topological states in quasicrystals, especially for: (1) Topological phases in three-dimensional quasicrystals
(2) Fragile topological phases in quasicrystals
(3) Multifractal nature of topological surface states
