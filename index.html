About me

I am a third-year master's student at Zhejiang University School of Physics. I feel very fortunate to have had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Professor Lih-King Lim since 2020. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Department of Energy and Power at Guangdong University of Technology in 2020.
